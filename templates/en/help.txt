Commands you can use:
<ul>
    <li>{$kill} [{$oldest}|{$youngest}] <i>(count)</i> {$from} {$yard} <i>(number)</i> - Kill sheep from specified yard, <i>count</i> - count of killed sheep, <i>youngest</i> - kill youngest sheep, <i>oldest</i> - kill oldest sheep(default). </li>
    <li>{$move}  <i>(count)</i> {$from} {$yard} <i>(number)</i> {$to} {$yard} <i>(number)</i> - Moving sheep from yard to yard
    <li>{$create} <i>(count)</i> {$in} {$yard} <i>(number)</i> - Create sheep in specified yard
    <li>{$refresh} - Refresh
</ul>
